Photo and Video Policy

No photography or videography is permitted at any time during the competition.

To protect all performers, families and participating schools, attendees must not take photos or record videos anywhere in the venue during the event.

We ask all guests to respect this policy fully. Anyone found photographing or recording may be asked to stop immediately and may be required to leave the venue.
